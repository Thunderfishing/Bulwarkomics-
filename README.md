# Bulwarkomics: The Phoenix Doctrine
**Tagline**: Forge a Debt-Free, Resilient America.  
**Summary**: The Phoenix Doctrine slashes debt with jubilees, swaps taxes for tariffs and excise, and builds a thorium-powered, co-op-driven nation—ready for crises or aliens. Proven by 2075: $50T GDP, 10% debt, leading an $85T Pact. Here’s how.

## Economic & Financial Reform
1. **Debt and Property Jubilee**  
   - *Reform*: Cash payments ($5,000 base, +$1,000/child, up to 3) every 5 years in polymer notes, initial goods (food, fuel).  
   - *Funded*: Tariffs & excise taxes.  
   - *Intent*: Slash household debt, break credit reliance, buffer shocks.

2. **Dual-Currency System (Polymer Notes)**  
   - *Reform*: Metal-infused notes ($1-$20, trace gold/silver), blockchain option, mandatory for transactions.  
   - *Funded*: Tariffs & excise taxes.  
   - *Intent*: Replace fiat, ensure durable value, reduce fragility.

3. **Treasury Control & Debt Discipline**  
   - *Reform*: Close Federal Reserve, Treasury issues notes; cap T-bills, 12% GDP debt ceiling (67% vote for emergencies), 10-year repayment.  
   - *Funded*: Tariffs & excise taxes.  
   - *Intent*: Limit debt, end central bank control, enforce discipline.

4. **Tariffs & Excise Tax System**  
   - *Reform*: Eliminate income/property taxes; 15-25% tariffs on imports, 10-25% excise on goods/services (exempt food, housing, utilities), rebates ($2,500/person for bottom 20%).  
   - *Funded*: Tariffs & excise taxes.  
   - *Intent*: Secure revenue, shift burden to consumption/trade, boost local production, ease low-income strain.

5. **Glass-Steagall & Credit Unions**  
   - *Reform*: Reinstate Glass-Steagall banking separation, Canadian-style credit unions (10-20 regions, low-interest note loans).  
   - *Funded*: Tariffs & excise taxes.  
   - *Intent*: Curb speculation, boost local finance, support co-ops/startups.

6. **Sovereign Wealth Funds**  
   - *Rainy Day Fund*: Invests in metals, rural infra, co-op bonds; 67% vote to tap beyond emergencies.  
   - *R&D Fund*: Funds energy (thorium), AI, biotech, manufacturing, STEM exchanges.  
   - *Funded*: Tariffs & excise taxes.  
   - *Intent*: Buffer crises, drive innovation, global ties.
